Setting up Arduino

The arduino will collect the data about surroundings.

The arduino will be connected to the computer via USB.

To set it up follow the diagram here: Arduino Diagram

All the necessary components are listed in the diagram.

Connecting to MindFlex

The MindFlex headset will collect data about your FOCUS / MEDITATION

The MindFlex headset will be connected to the computer via Bluetooth.

To connect -> open your bluetooth settings and look for HC-06

Integrating Eirene

Eirene is a web application that will help you to focus and meditate.

To integrate Eirene with the arduino and MindFlex headset, you need to follow the steps below:

  1. Connect your devices
  2. Login/Register on the Eirene page
  3. Download the Eirene PC app and follow the steps there
  4. Select a FOCUS / MEDITATION session on the user dashboard
